Chess Principles To Improve Your Game

This is a compilation of principles, guidelines and rules of thumb to aid your evaluation of chess positions and improve your play.

As always, it is vital to remember that these tips are not hard, universal rules. Their validity varies by position, and what separates a great player from a good one is the knowlage of when to bend or break them.
